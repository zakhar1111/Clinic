# Doctor's Clinic
Adoctor's clinic can allow many doctors to treat many patients. A patient can book an appointment with a doctor for their questions or treatment.

## Here's the list of requirements and features:
1. A patient will need to register on the system to make a booking. To register, they provide their name, email address, phone number, and government ID number.
2. When making a booking, the patient can indicate to see one of the many doctors available in the clinic.
3. Each doctor has a name and a short biography that patients can see.
4. A patient can see the available times for a doctor and choose a time to make a booking. Times are madein15-minute increments from 9AM unti l5PM every day of the week.
5. Doctor scan specify their working hours in the system, which will limit the ability for patients to make bookings: a patient can't book a specific doctor if that doctor is not working on that day.
6. The clinic admin staff can make bookings on the patient's behalf, if apatient walks into the clinic.
7. Anappointment has a specific price associated with it, which the patient pays after their appointment.The price depends on the doctor that was seen.
8. When the patient makes their payment, they will need to provide their government healthID number and their health insurance details, such as the name of the provider andt he membershipID.
